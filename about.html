About ThriveHub

Empowering educators to support our most vulnerable learners.

Welcome to ThriveHub, a dedicated resource center founded by Teresa Bruno. As a leader in education with a deep-rooted passion for student wellbeing, I created this platform to address a critical gap in the classroom: the move from simple "accommodation" to authentic inclusion.

Our Mission

We believe that every child deserves an environment that fits their needs. Our focus is on Wave One Mastery—providing universal supports that benefit all students, particularly those who are most vulnerable or neurodivergent.

Free & Accessible

Education is a right, and high-quality support strategies should be available to every teacher, regardless of their school's budget. ThriveHub is committed to providing easy-to-access resources at no cost to educators. From trauma-informed practices to interoception tools, everything here is designed to be implemented immediately in the classroom.

The Vision

ThriveHub isn't just a website; it's a movement toward a more compassionate, evidence-based approach to teaching. By equipping educators with the right tools, we ensure that no student is left behind and that every classroom becomes a place where every learner can truly thrive.
