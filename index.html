About our team

We provide Wave One intervention — proactive, school‑wide support to strengthen wellbeing and engagement for all students. We encourage leaders and staff to join our dedicated Microsoft Teams spaces for up‑to‑date information and practical resources on student wellbeing, disability support, attendance and truancy strategies.
