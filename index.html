About our team

Our team is dedicated to offering Wave One intervention, providing proactive, school-wide support to strengthen wellbeing and engagement for all students.

We highly encourage leadership and staff members to join our dedicated Microsoft Teams pages, which are regularly updated with the latest information and practical resources on student wellbeing, support for students with disabilities, and strategies for improving attendance and addressing truancy. These pages are designed to keep you informed and provide helpful tools for everyday challenges.

  • General | Wellbeing Connection | Microsoft Teams
  • General | SWD Connections- PH2 | Microsoft Teams
  • General | Social Work Truancy | Microsoft Teams

For additional resources and insights, please also visit our Student Wellbeing Blog — featuring a wealth of support materials for staff at all stages of their careers.

Student Support Services remains firmly committed to our goals around High-Performing Teams and to aligning our work with the Department for Education’s strategic direction. By focusing on effective service delivery, fostering collaboration, and supporting targeted interventions, we are dedicated to creating tangible, positive outcomes for your sites. We look forward to working closely with you, side by side, to deliver on these priorities.
